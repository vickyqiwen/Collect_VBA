
The step to change and refresh the date fromat.
 
Select Colomn.
Change format from General to DATE (short date).
Data ribbon and click Text to Columns.
Choose Delimited and click Next.
Uncheck all the delimiters and click Next.
Click Finish.
The number formatting of all the cells will update.
You may filter it now.

-------------------------
 Example:
 Short date                            06/05/2012
 Medium date                        5-Jun-12
 Long date 	                 Tuesday, June 5, 2012
